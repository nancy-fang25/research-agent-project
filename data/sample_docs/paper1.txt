Title: Retrieval-Augmented Generation

Abstract:
This paper introduces Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), a method that combines retrieval systems with large language models to improve factual accuracy.

Method:
The system first retrieves relevant documents from a knowledge base using vector similarity search. The retrieved content is then used as additional context for the language model during generation.

Results:
RAG improves factual correctness and reduces hallucination compared to standard language models.

Conclusion:
Retrieval augmentation is an effective approach for improving reliability in generative AI systems.
